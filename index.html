About Us

A house built on the belief that literature is forever

Founded by readers who became editors, Timeless Ink was born from a single conviction: that the right story, told with honesty and craft, does not merely entertain — it alters the reader permanently.

We are an independent literary press dedicated to fiction that grapples with the full weight of human experience. We work closely with every author we publish, treating each manuscript as a collaboration rather than a transaction.

From our offices we have introduced debut novelists to international audiences and championed established voices that the wider industry overlooked. We publish because we believe these books matter.

"A book that endures is not written for its season — it is written for every season that follows."

Submissions

We are reading. We are listening.

Timeless Ink accepts unsolicited submissions from unagented and agented authors alike. We read everything we receive — slowly, and with full attention.

We are currently open to literary fiction of all lengths, including novellas. We do not publish genre fiction, children's books, or non-fiction at this time.

Submission Guidelines

What to Send Query letter, 1–2 page synopsis, first 50 pages
Format PDF or DOCX, double-spaced, 12pt serif
Word Count 60,000 – 120,000 words
Response Time 10–14 weeks; all submissions acknowledged
Simultaneous Accepted — please notify us promptly if offered elsewhere
